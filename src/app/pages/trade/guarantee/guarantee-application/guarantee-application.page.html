Help & Guidance

What is a Bank Guarantee?

A bank guarantee is a commitment by the bank to pay a specified amount to a beneficiary if the applicant fails to fulfill their obligations.

SWIFT Processing

Cross-border guarantees require SWIFT messaging (MT760). Additional bank details will be required.

Governing Rules

URDG 758 applies to demand guarantees. ISP98 is used for standby letters of credit.
